Interpretation services

Consecutive interpretation

The ideal form of interpretation between an audience and a speaker. Typically, the interpreter relays the translated voice to the audience whenever a speaker makes a comment.

Simultaneous interpretation

Simultaneous interpretation occurs when an interpreter converts a message from one language to another in real time. Unlike consecutive interpreting, the speaker's natural flow is preserved, resulting in a relatively smooth output for the listeners. Interpreters sit in a separate booth, wear a headset, listen to the speaker, and speak the translation into a microphone, which is wirelessly broadcast to audience members' headsets. This service, also known as UN-style interpretation, necessitates skilled interpreters and specialized equipment, both of which I'm Translation can provide.

Relay interpretation

Relay interpretation relays and interprets the speaker's message to other interpreters who speak a common language (typically English) and then translate the message to the target audience in their own language.

Whispered interpretation or Chuchotage

It is a form of simultaneous interpretation that takes place in person-to-person format. Usually in the shape of a low-tone voice.

Video conference interpretation

Technology has become a staple means of communication. We provide video conference interpretation live or in person.

Escort/travel interpretation

During a visit, the interpreter will accompany the client and serve as an assistant or local expert. Ideal for business meetings, trips, and conferences in other countries.
